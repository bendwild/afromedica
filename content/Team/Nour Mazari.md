## Hey there!

I am deeply aware of the racial inequalities that present across all aspects of healthcare. This awareness inspires me to actively contribute to a healthcare system where everyone feels seen, heard, and valued, not only regardless of, but also with respect for racial and cultural differences, heritage, and background. 

As a psychiatry resident, I am driven by the conviction that mental health is a vital, yet too often overlooked, pillar of overall healthcare. In psychiatry and mental health care inclusion, empathy, and connection are central values that I consider inseprable from true quality care. With my background and experiences, I aspire to serve as a bridge between different worlds. 

Through my commitment to Afromedica, I stand with our powerful vision of expanding and improving access to fair, inclusive and culturally aware healthcare and thus contributing to a healthier, more equitable society for all.
