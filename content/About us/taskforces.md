Taskforces are the backbone of AfroMedica. Each group focuses on a specific domain and is coordinated by at least one lead and one co-lead, working collaboratively to ensure smooth operations and alignment with our mission. 

Our current taskforces include:

<div class="card-grid">

  <div class="card">
    <h3>(Vice-)President</h3>
    <p>The president and vice-president ensure AfroMedica stays aligned with its vision and values. Their responsibilities include:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Leading and preparing general assemblies</li>
      <li>Serving as a contact point for both external partners and internal members</li>
      <li>Monitoring objectives and progress across all teams</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>PR</h3>
    <p>PR is responsible for AfroMedica’s public image and internal communication flow. Their main responsibilities are: </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Managing social media and creating content (instagram, linkedin, facebook...)</li>
      <li>Handling messages and DMs and directing them to the right teams</li>
      <li>Designing visuals</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Finance</h3>
    <p>Handles all financial matters and ensures proper budgeting and documentation. Their responsibilities are: </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Processing payments and reimbursements</li>
      <li>Bookkeeping and administrative compliance</li>
      <li>Drafting price lists and managing invoices</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>AfroMedica Community</h3>
    <p>This taskforce focuses on building a strong internal network among AfroMedica members. Their responsibilities include:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Inviting members to events (eg. AfroMedica Talks</li>
      <li>Welcoming new members and overseeing board applications</li>
      <li>Keeping track of active members and turnover</li>
      <li>Managing announcements</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>AfroMedica Talks</h3>
    <p>This taskforce focuses organizes educational and public-facing events like podcasts and journal clubs. Their main responsibilities are:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Planning and scheduling speakers and topics</li>
      <li>Managing logistics and locations</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>AfroMedica Academy</h3>
    <p>This is AfroMedica’s educational arm, focused on capacity building and knowledge sharing. Their responsibilities are: </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Maintaining a resource library</li>
      <li>Designing education modules</li>
      <li>Collaborating with external experts</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Internal Admin</h3>
    <p>This taskforce ensures operational continuity and internal structure. Their main responsibilities include:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Maintaining SharePoint and contact lists</li>
      <li>Coordinating all the taskforces internal work</li>
      <li>Overseeing internal calendars and administrative inquiries</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Policy</h3>
    <p>This taskforce drives our mission to bring forth with structural and systemic change. Their responsibilities are: </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Monitoring the political landscape and policy trends</li>
      <li>Engaging with various institutions (IOM, Domus Medica, hospitals, Unia, EU...</li>
      <li>Writing opinion pieces for mainstream media</li>
      <li>Developing and publishing internal guidelines</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  
</div>
