So you're interested in joining the board of AfroMedica? Great!

The next step is figuring out where you fit in our team. We distributed our objectives into different **[[content/taskforces|taskforces]]**. Each taskforce has a specific set of tasks and responsibilities. Feel free to take your time to learn more about the different taskforces. 

If you feel at home in one of the taskforces you can press join which will take you to a google forms. Don't worry if you have more than one preference. You can opt for multiple taskforces in the google forms. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to [contact us](contact)!
