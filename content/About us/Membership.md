# Why become a member?
  
## 1. Be the first one to know about our events
By signing up as a member you will be the first one to be notified about our events. Besides being convenient, it will prove worthwhile with certain events with limited spots.  

## 2. Discounts on our events
Every member gets up to 5 euros discount on all of our events!

## 3. Member only events
Certain events are exclusively available for members. For example,  at the end of the academic year we invite every member to an exciting closing event. 

## 4. Get access to exclusive platforms
We run a private Facebook group where members can share their own experiences, explore unique insights and stay up to date with all the initiatives and organizations tackling this healthcare problem. A safe harbor but generally just a cozy space. 

## 5. Be part of the change
Do you agree with our mission and want to help make this become reality? Well, as a member, you're spreading the mindset and keeping the dream alive.

# What does it mean to be a member?
  
## 1. Attendance at meeting 
We ask of our members to attend at least one online or live AfroMedica meeting every semester

In doing so you get the chance to become more familiar with our inner workings and to give input on subjects that matter the most to you.   
A meeting typically lasts maximum 45 minutes.    ​

## 2. Participation in events or social media
We ask of our members to make a commitment in supporting AfroMedica. This can take many forms and is completely up to you!

We believe being a member of AfroMedica means taking part in our mission of transforming our current healthcare sector into an integer system that values and acknowledges differences and similarities. 

As a result, membership comes with a set of responsibilities but also with a lot of benefits!
