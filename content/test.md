## Who We Are

AfroMedica is a grassroots collective of healthcare professionals, students, and academics.  
We work to challenge racial disparities in the Belgian healthcare system through evidence-based knowledge, structural advocacy, and community care.

> [!info]+ Read more about our mission  
> [→ About AfroMedica](/about)

---

## What We Do

We operate at the intersection of **care, education, activism, and policy**.  
Our work spans journal clubs, podcast discussions, policy advocacy, and the development of learning materials.



> [!info]+ 👥 Who We Are  
> AfroMedica brings together medicine, policy, education, and activism to build a racially conscious healthcare system.  
> [→ Meet the team](/about)

> [!attention]+ 🛠️ What We Do  
> From policy work to education, we tackle systemic inequality through evidence, collaboration, and care.
