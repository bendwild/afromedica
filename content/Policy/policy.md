## What we do 

We are currently working on **policy recommendations, guidelines, and opinion pieces**, while keeping space for diverse initiatives that advance our mission.  

Our ultimate goal is to embed **anti-racism as a fundamental principle** within healthcare structures in Belgium and beyond.

Our work focuses on:
- Advocating for policy changes that address institutional racism in healthcare.  
- Collaborating with stakeholders to promote inclusive practices and equitable care.  
- Publishing guidelines, position papers, and opinion pieces that shape public and institutional awareness.
