<section class="about-talks container">
  <h2>About Afromedica Talks</h2>
  <p>
    Afromedica Talks hosts a journal club every <strong>second Wednesday of the month</strong>. Speakers deliver presentations of approximately 20 minutes on diverse social or medical-scientific topics, followed by a discussion session. All topics are evidence-based, with a focus on sharing scientific knowledge. We welcome everyone to participate and contribute to the conversation.
  </p>
</section>
